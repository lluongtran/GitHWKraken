NASA Typography Style Guide

Style Guide - Version 1

This is a style guide template for new projects. The template includes colours, typography, icons, buttons, fields, grid and images. You can download it for both Sketch and Photoshop.

03 Icons

The icons are from “Linea” and can be downloaded from linea.io You can get the icons in SVG, PNG or in font-format. They are licensed under CCBY and are free to use.



04 Buttons

Below you will find our buttons, in default, hover and active. Furthermore, you will see the three allowed colours for CTA buttons.



05 Fields

All fields should be shown with a label. Both the label, placeholder and border has the colour #999999. Other colours can be found in the sceme.



06 Grid

The grid is 12 column, and is 1170px with a gutter on 30px (15x2). Breakpoints will be 970px for tablet and 750px for mobile devices..



07 Images

Images are always 100% width of the container. Image captions/descriptions should always be 13px and placed directly under the image with a 10px margin from the image.
